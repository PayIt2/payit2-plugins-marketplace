### PayIt2 Campaign Coach

Your AI coach for everything on PayIt2 — fundraising campaigns, event ticketing, and group payment collection.

**Commands:**
- `/launch-campaign` — Build a fundraiser from scratch with story, title, goal, and launch strategy
- `/plan-event` — Set up an event with ticket tiers, early bird pricing, and promotion timeline
- `/collect-from-group` — Create a group collection with cost splitting and automated reminders
- `/weekly-checkin` — Weekly health check with content calendar and action items
- `/boost-campaign` — Diagnose and rescue a stalled campaign
- `/thank-donors` — Generate personalized thank-you messages at scale

**Example prompts:**
- *"Help me raise $15,000 for my neighbor's family after a house fire."*
- *"I'm organizing a charity golf tournament for 120 people."*
- *"I need to collect $200 from 25 teammates for new uniforms."*
