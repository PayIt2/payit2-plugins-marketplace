# Collection Templates

Ready-to-use message templates for every stage of a group collection. Replace bracketed placeholders with your specific details.

---

## Launch Messages by Channel

### Group Text / iMessage

> Hey [group name]! We're collecting for [purpose]. Each person owes $[amount]. Deadline is [date]. Here's the link to pay: [PayIt2 link]. Takes 30 seconds. Thanks!

**Tone notes**: Keep it short. Texts get skimmed. One link, one amount, one deadline. No walls of text.

### Email

> **Subject: [Group Name] — [Purpose] Payment ($[amount] due by [date])**
>
> Hi everyone,
>
> I'm collecting payments for [purpose]. Here are the details:
>
> - **What**: [Brief description of what the funds cover]
> - **Amount**: $[amount] per person
> - **Deadline**: [date]
> - **Pay here**: [PayIt2 link]
>
> [1-2 sentences about what happens with the funds — "This covers our cabin rental and group meals for the weekend."]
>
> If you have any questions, just reply to this email. Thanks!
>
> [Your name]

**Tone notes**: Professional but warm. Bullet points make it scannable. Subject line includes the dollar amount and deadline so people know what to expect before opening.

### Facebook / Social Media Post

> [Group name] — time to pay for [purpose]! 🎉
>
> 💰 $[amount] per person
> 📅 Deadline: [date]
> 🔗 Pay here: [PayIt2 link]
>
> [1 sentence about what it covers.] Questions? Drop a comment or DM me.

**Tone notes**: Casual and upbeat. Emojis are appropriate for social platforms. Keep it to a glance.

### Slack / Teams Message

> **@channel — [Purpose] Collection**
>
> Collecting $[amount] per person for [purpose]. Deadline: [date].
>
> → **Pay here**: [PayIt2 link]
>
> Details: [1-2 sentences explaining what the funds cover and why the deadline matters.]
>
> Already paid? Ignore this. Questions? Thread below.

**Tone notes**: Direct and organized. Use the channel mention once (for the launch only). Thread for questions keeps the channel clean.

## Reminder Templates by Stage

### Day 3 — Progress Update

> Quick update on [purpose] — we're at [X]% of our goal! Thanks to everyone who's already paid. If you haven't yet, here's the link: [PayIt2 link]. Deadline is [date].

**Tone notes**: Lead with gratitude and progress. The percentage creates social proof. Don't repeat all the details from the launch message.

### Day 7 / Midpoint — Momentum Update

> Over halfway there! [X] of [Y] people have paid for [purpose]. If you haven't had a chance yet, the link is here: [PayIt2 link]. We need everyone in by [date] so we can [real constraint — book the venue, place the order, etc.].

**Tone notes**: Concrete numbers ("12 of 18") are more motivating than percentages at this stage. Connect the deadline to an action you need to take.

### 3 Days Before Deadline — Urgency

> Heads up — deadline for [purpose] is [date], which is [X] days away. We still need [X] people to pay so we can [action tied to deadline]. If you haven't paid yet, please do so here: [PayIt2 link]. Thanks!

**Tone notes**: Urgency without panic. State the consequence clearly but calmly. "So we can book the venue" is better than "or we'll lose our reservation!"

### Deadline Day — Final Nudge

> Today's the last day to pay for [purpose]. We need $[remaining amount] more to hit our goal. If you haven't paid, please take a minute now: [PayIt2 link]. Thank you to everyone who's contributed!

**Tone notes**: Short and direct. No guilt. Combine the ask with a thank-you to those who have already paid.

### Post-Deadline — Straggler Follow-Up (Private Message Only)

> Hey [name], just following up on the [purpose] collection. I know things get busy — if you're still planning to pay, the link is here: [PayIt2 link]. I'm finalizing everything by [grace date], so any time before then works. Let me know if you have questions!

**Tone notes**: Warm, understanding, one-on-one. Never send this to the group. Give a specific grace date (2-3 days after the original deadline). If someone can't pay, be gracious — offer to work something out.

## Category-Specific Launch Messages

### Team Dues

> Hey [team name]! Season dues are $[amount] per player, covering [what dues cover — field rental, equipment, ref fees, etc.]. Deadline to pay is [date].
>
> Pay here: [PayIt2 link]
>
> If you have any issues with the amount or need to set up a payment plan, DM me. Looking forward to a great season!

### Trip Collection

> [Group name] trip is happening! Here's the cost breakdown:
>
> - [Tier 1, e.g., "Shared room"]: $[amount]
> - [Tier 2, e.g., "Private room"]: $[amount]
> - [Tier 3, e.g., "Day pass (no room)"]: $[amount]
>
> This covers [what's included — lodging, group meals, activities, etc.]. Pay by [date] so I can lock in our reservation.
>
> Pay here: [PayIt2 link]

### Group Gift

> We're putting together a group gift for [recipient] for [occasion]! The suggested contribution is $[amount], but any amount is welcome.
>
> **Important**: Please don't mention this to [recipient]!
>
> Contribute here: [PayIt2 link]
>
> I'll share a photo of the gift once we have it. Thanks for chipping in!

### Event Costs

> Time to split the costs for [event name]! Here's what we're covering:
>
> - Venue: $[amount]
> - Catering: $[amount]
> - [Other items]: $[amount]
> - **Total**: $[total] → **$[per person] per person**
>
> Please pay by [date] so I can confirm everything with the vendors.
>
> Pay here: [PayIt2 link]

### School / Classroom Fees

> Hi [class/school] parents! We're collecting $[amount] per student for [purpose — field trip, class supplies, end-of-year party, etc.].
>
> Here's what it covers:
> - [Item 1]: $[amount]
> - [Item 2]: $[amount]
> - [Item 3]: $[amount]
>
> Please pay by [date]. If the cost is a hardship, please reach out to me privately — we want every student to participate.
>
> Pay here: [PayIt2 link]

**Tone notes**: Always include a hardship note for school collections. Inclusivity matters.

## Completion & Thank-You Messages

### Goal Reached — Celebration

> We did it! 🎉 100% collected for [purpose]. Thank you to everyone who paid and helped make this happen.
>
> [1-2 sentences about next steps — "I'll be placing the order this week" or "The cabin is officially booked!"]
>
> You all are the best. [Group name] for the win!

**Tone notes**: Celebrate the group, not yourself. Keep it short and positive. Share what happens next so people see the result of their contribution.

### Thank-You / Receipt Summary

> **[Purpose] Collection — Final Summary**
>
> - **Total collected**: $[amount]
> - **Number of contributors**: [X]
> - **What it's being used for**:
>   - [Item 1]: $[amount]
>   - [Item 2]: $[amount]
>   - [Item 3]: $[amount]
>
> [Any overage/remaining balance note, e.g., "The extra $30 will go toward next season's fund."]
>
> Thanks again to everyone who contributed. If you have any questions about how the funds were used, just ask.

**Tone notes**: Transparency builds trust for future collections. People are more likely to pay quickly next time if they see exactly where their money went. Send this after the funds have been spent or allocated.
